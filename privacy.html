Privacy Policy for OpenBarTab Last Updated: March 22, 2025 Introduction This Privacy Policy describes how OpenBarTab ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application OpenBarTab (the "App"), which is provided as a free service supported by advertisements. Information We Collect Location Data The App collects precise location data, including background location, to detect when you enter or leave bar venues. This is essential for the core functionality of notifying you about your open bar tab when you leave a venue. Device Information We collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, to ensure proper app functionality. Usage Data We collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you use and the time spent in the App, to improve our service. Advertising Data To support our free service, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process information about you, including: Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID or Apple's IDFA) IP address Device information Approximate location data (if precise location permission is not granted) Information about your interactions with advertisements How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: Detect when you enter or leave a bar Send you notifications about your bar tab Improve the App's functionality Troubleshoot issues and debug the App Serve relevant advertisements Analyze app performance and usage patterns Information Sharing and Disclosure Third-Party Advertising We partner with Google AdMob to show advertisements in the App. Google AdMob may use the collected information to personalize advertisements shown to you. Their use of your information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy. Other Disclosures We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy, including: To comply with legal obligations To enforce our terms and policies In connection with a sale or merger of our company To our service providers who assist us in operating the App Data Retention We retain your location data for only as long as necessary to provide the App's services. Your location history is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers. Your Rights and Choices You can: Stop all collection of information by the App by uninstalling it Disable location access through your device settings (note that this will impact the core functionality of the App) Opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking, Android: Settings > Google > Ads) Reset your advertising identifier through your device settings Receive a copy of your data by contacting us at mail@voghtonline.com Children's Privacy The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the App or provide any information to us. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
