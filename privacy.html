# Privacy Policy for OpenBarTab **Last Updated: March 19, 2025** ## Introduction This Privacy Policy describes how OpenBarTab ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application OpenBarTab (the "App"). ## Information We Collect ### Location Data The App collects precise location data, including background location, to detect when you enter or leave bar venues. This is essential for the core functionality of notifying you about your open bar tab when you leave a venue. ### Device Information We collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, to ensure proper app functionality. ### Usage Data We collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you use and the time spent in the App, to improve our service. ## How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: - Detect when you enter or leave a bar - Send you notifications about your bar tab - Improve the App's functionality - Troubleshoot issues and debug the App ## Information Sharing and Disclosure We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as described below: - To comply with legal obligations - To enforce our terms and policies - In connection with a sale or merger of our company ## Data Retention We retain your location data for only as long as necessary to provide the App's services. Your location history is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers. ## Your Rights and Choices You can: - Stop all collection of information by the App by uninstalling it - Disable location access through your device settings (note that this will impact the core functionality of the App) - Receive a copy of your data by contacting us at mail@voghtonline.com ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. ## Contact Us If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: mail@voghtonline.com
